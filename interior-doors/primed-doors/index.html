White Primed Interior Doors

Doceian white primed interior doors arrive paint-ready — factory primed for on-site finishing. The right choice for builders, finishing contractors, and millwork shops that specify paint-grade interior doors.

Primed doors

Factory primed, field painted

White primed doors reduce on-site prep time. The factory-applied primer creates a uniform base that accepts paint evenly — ideal for high-volume residential and light commercial projects.

Why primed doors

Less prep time, more consistent results.

Factory-applied primer

Every door leaves the factory with a uniform white primer coat — no sanding or spot priming required on site.

Flexible color specification

Primed doors let builders and homeowners choose any paint color at the time of finishing, not at the time of order.

Molded Interior Doors

Doceian molded interior doors combine attractive panel profiles with practical, durable construction. Suitable for apartments, homes, restaurants, hotels, and any high-volume residential or commercial project.

Molded doors

Smooth panel construction

Molded panel doors are pressed from engineered wood composite for a consistent, paint-ready surface. The profile is clean and compatible with standard casing and hardware.

Why molded doors

A practical choice for volume projects.

Consistent profile

Engineered construction means every door comes off spec — no warping variation between units.

Paint-ready surface

The smooth face takes primer and paint evenly, reducing on-site finishing time.

High-volume friendly

Molded doors are ideal for multi-unit residential, hospitality, and repeat builder programs.
